\chapter{Controling Penguin Effecs in \phis}
\label{Penguins}

As explained in \secref{TheBsJpsiKstDecay} controling higher order effects becomes mandatory in
future flavor physics experiments. The issue of estimating contribution from penguin topologies
to the total \BsJpsiPhi amplitude is addressed in the current chapter. The key element of which
is to involve as many similar to \BsJpsiPhi chanells in an attempt to maximie the sensitivity on
the parameters that describe the above mentioned penguin contributions. In sections \secref{jpsiphi_amp_struct}
to \secref{penguin_more_chanells} the fundamentals of the necessay formalism are introduced.
Whereas the details of the penguin paramter estimation are explained in \secref{penguin_more_chanells}.
Results are given in \secref{penguin_results}. Note that the strategy followed in this chapter
as well as the quoted equations are suggested by ~\cite{Fleischer:1999zi,Faller:2008gt,DeBruyn:2014oga}.
